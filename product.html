Forge Legend

Forge Legend is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG). Set in the fictional world of Arendahl, Forge Legend allows players to create avatar-style characters and explore a sprawling universe while interacting with nonreal players—called nonplayer characters (NPCs)—and other real-world players (PCs).
